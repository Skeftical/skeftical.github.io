Fotis Savva

Applied Machine Learning

I am a Principal Member of Technical Staff at Oracle, specializing in applied Machine Learning solutions for MySQL HeatWave.
I hold a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Glasgow, where I was a member of the Information, Data & Analysis group. My work focused on developing innovative solutions to enhance data exploration and expedite data computations using Machine Learning. Prior to my PhD, I graduated with a BSc (Honors 1st Class) in Computer Science from the University of Glasgow.
